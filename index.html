What is Trézor.io/Start?

Trézor.io/Start is the official onboarding platform for all Trézor hardware wallets, including the Trézor Model One and the Trézor Model T. This portal provides a secure and user-friendly setup process, guiding users through firmware installation, wallet creation, and essential security practices.

Why is this important? In the cryptocurrency space, counterfeit wallets and phishing websites are common. By starting at the official Trézor.io/Start site, you ensure that your device is genuine, secure, and updated with the latest firmware. This minimizes the risk of falling victim to malicious attacks, keeping your digital assets safe and under your control.
